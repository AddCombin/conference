The conference will take place at the Institute of Mathematics and Scientific Computing of the University of Graz. The address of the building is Heinrichstraße 36 (building 11 of the university campus).

All talks will take place in the big lecture halls on the ground floor. The rooms are equipped with projectors and big chalkboards.
